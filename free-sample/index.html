Frequently asked

What do I actually get?

A CSV with 25 rows. Each row is a real prospect (verified email, LinkedIn URL, firmographics) plus a personalized subject line and opener grounded in a recent, verifiable signal about their company. You also get the full draft email — you can ship it as-is or rewrite the body.

What does the 24h delivery mean?

We process requests Monday through Friday. Submit before 4pm ET and your sample lands within 24 hours. After-hours and weekend requests deliver by end of the next business day. Sometimes faster — we batch them as they come in.

Is there really no catch?

No catch. We send the sample because it's a much better pitch than the landing page is — when someone sees personalized openers for prospects they actually want, the paid tiers sell themselves. About 1-in-4 sample requesters becomes a paid customer within two weeks. You're under zero obligation. We do follow up once after delivery to ask if it was useful — reply "no thanks" and we stop.

What if my ICP is unusual?

Tell us in the form. We default to US/CA/UK and 1–200 employee companies, but we have coverage for ~190 countries and most B2B sub-verticals. If your ICP is a sub-100-company global niche, we'll tell you up front rather than waste both our time.

How is this different from Apollo, Clay, or ZoomInfo?

Those give you databases — you build the openers. We deliver the prospect and the personalized opener already written, grounded in a public signal we cite verbatim. See the side-by-sides at Compare.

Can I get more than 10 in the free sample?

No — 10 is the cap. If you want 50, that's the Starter tier at $97. If you want 250, that's Growth at $297. The free sample exists to prove quality; the paid tiers exist for volume.

What if the sample is bad?

Reply to the delivery email with what's off — wrong vertical, opener reads templated, signal is stale — and we redo the bad rows. If the whole batch misses, we redo the whole batch. You haven't paid anything; the cost is ours.
