How the desk works

Pipeline Desk is not another sending platform. It is the recurring research and personalization layer that feeds the sender you already use.

01

Lock the ICP

We turn your product, proof, excludes, geos, and buyer titles into a weekly desk brief with no broad scrape targets.

02

Build the pipeline

The engine pulls matched accounts, verifies contacts, finds recent public signals, and drafts a first-touch email per prospect.

03

Drop every week

You get a CSV and a short desk note: what changed, which segment was strongest, and which angle to send first.

04

Refine from replies

Reply snippets, booked calls, bounces, and rejections feed the next desk brief so the pipeline narrows toward what converts.
