Outbound-in-a-Box vs ZoomInfo

ZoomInfo is an enterprise-tier B2B data + sales-engagement platform — typically $15,000+/year minimum, built for teams of 5+ AEs/SDRs. Outbound-in-a-Box is a $97-$497 done-for-you prospect CSV with personalized openers per row, built for solo founders and small teams. These aren't competitive products — they serve wildly different customers. Here's the honest read.

TL;DR

Choose ZoomInfo if you have a 5+ person sales team, a $15k+/year tooling budget, need a deep enterprise-grade database (430M+ contacts, intent data, org charts, technographics), and can dedicate ops resources to actually use what you're paying for.

Choose Outbound-in-a-Box if you're a solo founder or 1-5 person team, don't have a SaaS-tooling budget that big, and want a done-for-you CSV with personalized openers per row in 24h for $97 — no contract, no negotiation, no Salesloft-style onboarding.

They're not really comparable. Most teams choosing between them are actually choosing between two different stages of company. If you're at the size where ZoomInfo's per-seat pricing makes sense, you also have the team to make the data useful. If you're not at that size, ZoomInfo will burn money sitting unused.

What each one does

ZoomInfo — enterprise B2B data + sales-engagement platform

ZoomInfo (zoominfo.com) is the largest B2B database in the market — 430M+ verified contacts, deep firmographics, intent data (signals from across the web indicating buyer research activity), technographics (what tools each company uses), org charts, and direct dials. On top of the data they offer sales-engagement (sequencing, dialer), marketing-side products (chat, account intelligence), and integrations with every major CRM.

What ZoomInfo gives you: the most comprehensive B2B dataset available, plus a full sales-engagement layer. What it costs: typically $15,000-$80,000+/year minimum, with annual contracts, professional services for implementation, and an SDR/Ops team needed to extract real value from the spend.

Outbound-in-a-Box — done-for-you signal-grounded lists

Outbound-in-a-Box delivers a CSV: 50–500 prospects per batch, each with a personalized cold-email opener grounded in a verifiable public signal about the prospect's company. We do the prospect sourcing (via Vibe Prospecting / Explorium), the signal research, and the per-prospect opener writing. The CSV is delivered as an attachment from a real human; no implementation, no onboarding, no annual contract.

What we give you: the research + opener layer pre-done, on a per-batch or monthly basis. What we don't give you: an enterprise database, intent data, technographics, dialer, or sales-engagement platform.

When to pick which

ZoomInfo is the right pick if…

  • You have a 5+ person sales team where deep firmographic search + intent data + technographics + dialer all get used.
  • Your annual revenue justifies $15-80k/yr in tooling — the rule of thumb is ~1% of ARR on sales stack.
  • You have an ops/RevOps person to manage data hygiene, CRM sync, and seat allocation.
  • Your sales motion needs phone outreach in addition to email — ZoomInfo's direct dials are best-in-class.
  • You're already running multi-channel outbound at scale and just need a bigger / cleaner data source.

Outbound-in-a-Box is the right pick if…

  • You're a solo founder, fractional GTM person, or 1-5 person team — ZoomInfo's per-seat pricing doesn't pencil.
  • Your buyer is researchable from public sources (funding announcements, ProductHunt, LinkedIn) — you don't need ZoomInfo-tier depth.
  • You'd rather pay $97 per batch + skip the annual contract negotiation + skip the 4-8 week implementation.
  • Email is your only or primary cold channel — you don't need a dialer or full sales-engagement platform.
  • You want the opener-writing labor done for you, not a bigger dataset to drown in.

An honest caveat

If your sales motion legitimately requires enterprise-grade data depth — multi-thread account selling, intent-data-driven ABM, hundreds-of-line org-chart navigation — ZoomInfo is the right call and we are NOT a substitute. Outbound-in-a-Box is built for the founder-led, single-buyer-per-account world. Past Series B with a real sales team, ZoomInfo's value compounds; below that, it's overspend.
