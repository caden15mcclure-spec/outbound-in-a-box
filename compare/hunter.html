Outbound-in-a-Box vs Hunter

Hunter.io is a B2B email-finder + verifier with a cold-email sender layered on top. Outbound-in-a-Box delivers a done-for-you CSV of 50-500 prospects with personalized signal-grounded openers per row. They solve different parts of the outbound stack — Hunter solves the "do these emails exist" problem; we solve the "what do I write to each prospect" problem.

TL;DR

Choose Hunter alone if you already have a prospect list (from LinkedIn search or scraping) and just need to find + verify the email addresses for those people, OR if you want a lightweight all-in-one (find email + warm + send) for a small program.

Choose Outbound-in-a-Box alongside Hunter if you want done-for-you prospects + openers in CSV form, and don't want to do the LinkedIn-search-then-Hunter-verify step yourself. We deliver verified emails as part of the CSV; you don't need a separate verifier on top.

They sit at different layers. Hunter is best when you have a prospect list and need emails; we're best when you don't want to assemble the prospect list at all and want openers done too.

What each one does

Hunter.io — email finder, verifier, lightweight sender

Hunter.io (hunter.io) is one of the longest-running B2B email tools — domain-based email search, individual email finder, email verifier, and a Campaigns product for cold-email sending. Pricing starts at a free tier (25 searches/month) and scales to $49-$199/month depending on volume.

What Hunter gives you: a way to find and verify business email addresses, plus a basic sender. What it doesn't give you: prospect curation (you bring the names + companies), opener writing, or signal research.

Outbound-in-a-Box — done-for-you signal-grounded lists

Outbound-in-a-Box delivers a CSV: 50–500 prospects per batch, each with verified email + LinkedIn URL + firmographics + a personalized opener grounded in a real public signal. We handle prospect sourcing, signal research, opener writing, and email verification — all packaged in a CSV.

We don't have a sender layer. You import the CSV into Hunter Campaigns / Lemlist / Smartlead / your own Gmail. Email verification is included in the CSV — you don't need a separate verifier.

When to pick which

Hunter alone is the right pick if…

  • You already have prospect names + companies (from LinkedIn Sales Nav, an existing CRM, or scraping) and just need to find + verify the email addresses.
  • You want one tool that does email-finder + verify + simple sending without juggling 3 separate tools.
  • Your sending volume is small (<100/day) and you don't need a polished sequencer like Lemlist.
  • You're writing the openers yourself or have an SDR who is — you don't need them done for you.
  • Your budget is tight ($0-$50/month) and you'll do the prospecting + opener-writing work in-house.

Outbound-in-a-Box alongside Hunter is the right pick if…

  • You'd rather skip the LinkedIn-search-then-Hunter-verify step and have the whole prospect curated for you in 24h.
  • Hunter's email-finder is fine but you're stuck on "what do I actually write to these people?" — that's the gap we close.
  • You like Hunter Campaigns for the simple sending but want the openers already-written rather than mail-merge.
  • Hunter's database isn't deep enough for your niche-vertical ICP — we source per-batch via Vibe Prospecting which has different coverage.
  • You want a refund guarantee on opener quality (Hunter can't guarantee what you write).

An honest caveat

Hunter's free tier is genuinely useful for very small operations. If you're sending under 25 cold emails/month and writing your own openers, you don't need us — Hunter alone will do the job. We make sense once the bottleneck becomes "I don't have time to write 50 personalized openers" rather than "I don't have the email addresses."
