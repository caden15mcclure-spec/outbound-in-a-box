4. Quality standard

Every opener will be grounded in a real, public, recent signal (typically ≤6 months old) about the specific prospect's company. We cite the signal verbatim in the CSV so you can verify it. If any opener in your batch reads as templated mail-merge — i.e. could plausibly have been generated without actually looking at that prospect — that's a quality miss and you're entitled to a re-run of the bad row(s) or a refund (Section 5).

5. Refund policy

We refund 100%, no questions, in any of these cases:

  1. Within 48 hours of delivery, you report that one or more openers do not appear grounded in real signals (the openers read like generic mail-merge).
  2. The CSV is delivered late beyond the tier's stated SLA + a 12-hour grace window, AND you request a refund within 24 hours of receiving the late delivery.
  3. The ICP fit is wrong in a way we should have caught (e.g. you specified Series B SaaS founders and we surfaced agency owners across the board), AND you decline our one-round revision offer.

We do not refund for:

  • Open rates, reply rates, or meeting rates. These depend on your sending domain, warmup, follow-up sequence, day/time, copy iteration, and offer–market fit — none of which we control.
  • A subset of prospects bouncing or being unreachable (industry-standard CSVs include some bounce; we target <5% bounce rate).
  • You changing your ICP after delivery and wanting a re-run.

How to request: reply to your delivery email with the single word refund and a one-line reason. We process within 1 business day via the original payment method.
